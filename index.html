What is Fan Token?

Fan Token is a decentralized platform that allows artists to tokenize their music as NFTs and sell them directly to fans, ensuring fair compensation and fostering a closer connection between artists and their audience.

How do I buy music NFTs?

To buy music NFTs, you need to register on the Fan Token platform, browse the marketplace, and use $FAN Tokens to purchase the NFTs of your choice.

How do artists earn royalties?

Artists earn royalties from the initial sale of their NFTs and every subsequent play of their music on the platform and third-party platforms. The blockchain ensures transparent and automated royalty distribution.

What are $FAN Tokens?

$FAN Tokens are the native currency of the Fan Token platform, used for buying NFTs, supporting artists, and participating in various activities within the ecosystem.

How is the token supply distributed?

The total supply of 1 billion $FAN Tokens is distributed as follows: 60% for the ecosystem, 10% for PR, 10% for investors, 10% for R&D, and 10% for the team.

What is the roadmap for Fan Token?

Our roadmap includes four key phases: NFT Marketplace Start, Major Exchange Listing, Metaverse Platform, and Go Global. Each phase is designed to enhance the platform and expand our reach.
